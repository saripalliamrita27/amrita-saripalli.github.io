The Challenge

Allied Solutions provides insurance and financial technology to credit unions across the US. Their Lending Insights platform is a business intelligence tool designed to help institutions manage their lending operations. However, the legacy reporting system was so complex and unintuitive that lending teams heavily relied on internal support staff just to generate their monthly reports, causing severe workflow delays.

Our team was tasked with reimagining the custom reporting experience for their new "Greenfield" platform, eliminating manual work and integrating AI-driven insights to empower lenders to build reports independently.

Phase 1: Research & Audit

We began with a Heuristic Evaluation of the legacy software. We found that the system violated the "Visibility of System Status" heuristic because users couldn't see the results of their filtering actions instantly. The terminology was also ambiguous (e.g., labels like "Custom 10"), violating the "Match Between System and Real World" heuristic.

// Primary Research Insights

  • Opaque Configuration: The process of setting up a report was completely hidden and disconnected from the results.
  • AI as an Enhancement: Users didn't want a generic chatbot. They wanted AI seamlessly integrated into the report-building workflow, such as an AI forecasting tool directly on data visualizations.
  • Expert Needs: Advanced finance professors emphasized the need for real-time visual cues (like green/red risk scales) and clear entry points to control variables easily.

Phase 2: Ideation & Concept Validation

Following a blue-sky ideation and whiteboarding session, we developed two distinct low-fidelity prototypes (Version 1A and 1B) for A/B testing with Subject Matter Experts (SMEs).

Prototype A was designed for novice users with a simplified, dashboard-first approach. Prototype B targeted experienced users with a Tableau-inspired builder and a bento-style grid. The SME feedback revealed that users wanted a blend of both: the structured layout of Prototype B but the clear, pre-populated templates of Prototype A.

Phase 3: The Greenfield Solution

The final High-Fidelity prototype combined the most effective elements into Version 2.0. We implemented a unified dashboard where users can start from scratch, use templates, or generate a report entirely using an AI prompt.

Key AI Features Integrated:

  • AI Permissions Modal: To build trust, a first-time user experience explicitly asks for consent before AI accesses platform metrics.
  • AI Forecasting: Added directly to charts. Users input a date range, and the AI analyzes trends to project forward-looking data points onto the visualization.
  • AI Report Summaries: Users can generate an editable executive summary that highlights key metrics, detailed analysis, and actionable recommendations based on their chosen widgets.

Measurable Impact

To validate our design, we ran task-based usability tests comparing the legacy system against our redesigned Greenfield platform. The results were staggering:

  • Task Completion Time: Decreased by an average of 62.8% (from 172 seconds to 55.5 seconds).
  • Click Count: Reduced by 40.6% (from an average of 35 clicks to 20 clicks).

The redesign successfully eliminated cognitive overload, ensuring users could confidently manage their own reporting without support.
