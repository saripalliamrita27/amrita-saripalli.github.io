I'm Amrita Saripalli, a second-year UX Design student at Purdue University pursuing a Bachelor's degree with a minor in Computer & Information Technology. I sit at the intersection of design, technology, and human behavior — driven by a belief that the best digital experiences feel invisible.

My work spans enterprise analytics platforms, AI-powered marketing tools, and museum exhibit interactives. Whether I'm conducting user interviews, running usability tests, or building high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, I bring rigorous research thinking and a keen aesthetic sensibility to every project.

Outside the studio, I paint, play badminton, explore new music, and watch TV with my twin sister. That creative energy feeds directly into how I think about design — with curiosity, play, and a deep respect for the people on the other side of the screen.
